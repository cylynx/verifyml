Model Card for Credit Risk Model

Model Details

Overview

The company wants to automate the loan eligibility process based on customer details provided during the online application process. Customers assessed with healthy credit risk score will be passed to internal team for follow-up.

name: v1

    Considerations

    Intended Users

    • Loan assessment team and customers taking out a loan

    Use Cases

    • Save time in customer screening and reduce the rate of loan defaults.

    Fairness Considerations

    • Group at risk: gender and marital status
      Benefits: Customers can get quicker loan approvals. A more accurate model than current business rules will also enable more customers to apply for loans.
      Harms: Customers who are incorrectly predicted with high credit risk score will be denied access to loans.
      Mitigation Strategy: Remove the protected attributes from model training if they are insignificant based on model feature importance.
