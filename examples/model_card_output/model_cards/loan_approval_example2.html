Model Card for Loan Approval Model, outcome probability threshold = 0.45

Model Details

Overview

The company wants to automate the loan eligibility process based on customer details provided during the online application process. Binary prediction problem (approved or rejected). Customers assessed with healthy credit score will be passed to internal team for follow-up.

    Considerations

    Intended Users

    • Loan assessment team and customers taking out a loan

    Use Cases

    • Save time in customer screening and reduce the rate of loan defaults.

    Fairness Considerations

    • Group at risk: gender and marital status
      Benefits: Customers can get quicker loan approvals. A more accurate model than current business rules will also enable more customers to apply for loans.
      Harms: Customers who are falsely rejected (false-positive) will be denied access to loans.
      Mitigation Strategy: Adjust the outcome probability threshold such that not only it optimizes the business objectives but also reduce fairness disparity among the subgroups in the sensitive attributes. Refer to ROC curve analysis and adjust sample weights if necessary.
